STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE

About Strawberry Shortcake

An American preschool direct-to-video animated series produced by DIC Entertainment Corporation and American Greetings based on the franchise of the same name that debuted in March 2003. The series consists of 45 episodes, plus one short and one movie.

Strawberry Shortcake's characteristics

  1. She's full of big possibilities.
  2. She's loves adventure and believes you can do anything if you try
  3. She's a berry good friend who's already to help.
  4. She's kind and smart, and she loves her pets.
